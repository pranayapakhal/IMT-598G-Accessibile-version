IMT 598G – Accessible Course Page Sample

Instructor: Pranaya Pakala
Quarter: Winter 2026 • Credits: 4

Graduate-level Hybrid delivery Project-based

Course overview

This sample page demonstrates accessible HTML structure including headings, descriptive link text, tables with proper markup, semantic regions, and keyboard-friendly focus states. It is designed as a lightweight example of how course information can be presented in a way that supports a diverse range of learners and assistive technologies.

Learning outcomes

By the end of the course, students will be able to:

  • Identify common barriers in digital course materials and platforms.
  • Apply WCAG-aligned practices to course web content and documents.
  • Collaborate with campus partners to support inclusive learning experiences.
